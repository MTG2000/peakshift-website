Front End Developer

Introduction

We're looking for an awesome person to join our team as Front End Developer at our growing digital agency. You will be tasked with converting designs to clean, semantic HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

We are looking for someone who is proactive, eager to learn and develop their career, passionate about Front End Development and not to mention a design perfectionist.

Skills Required

  • Strong Knowledge of HTML5, & CSS3 (including SASS)
  • Experence with CSS3 Animations
  • Knowledge and Experience with Javascript / jQuery
  • Familiarity with Twitter Bootstrap
  • Ability to communicate effectively in English

Extra points for experience with atleast one MV* framework like Angular, Vue-JS, React.js etc and/or WordPress templating.

We use Sketch for UI design, Git for version control and SASS.

This is a fulltime position. You will be working remotely and will be communicating with the rest of the team in different time-zones over Skype and Slack. You may also have to adjust your schedules to accomodate for the time difference but we are pretty flexible too.
